Remove the Front Panel (DK2)

Remove the front panel to access to the or the Leap Motion board.

  1. If you have not already done so, Disconnect the HMD from the [%=General.Backtop%].
  2. Remove the front pad.
  3. Loosen the HMD headstrap.
  4. Remove the two outer screws (one on each side).

    For example:

  5. Remove the two screws near where the front pad resides (one on each side).

    For example:

    Click to enlarge

  6. Remove the two screws in the middle.

    For example:

    Click to enlarge

  7. Remove the screw at the front, center of the HMD.

    For example:

    Click to enlarge

  8. Confirm you have removed all seven screws.
  9. Detach the front panel.
