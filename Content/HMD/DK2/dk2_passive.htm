DK2

The DK2 HMD was an early prototype. It was equipped at the Lindon and the NY (Madam Tussaud’s) VECS. As of June 2017, DK2s were removed and CV1s with active tracking were equipped instead.

Beside using passive tracking, the design of the DK2 differed from later prototypes (CV1).
