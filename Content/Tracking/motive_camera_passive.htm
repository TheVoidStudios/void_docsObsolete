Calibrate the Stage Cameras

A full calibration of the cameras is required for a newly-built stage or if a camera has been added, moved or removed from an existing stage. If a full calibration is not required, follow the instructions to Refine Stage Calibration in Motive.
It is recommended that you have two people for this procedure. One working with the Motive software and the other to perform the wanding on the stage.

In this topic:

  • Prepare for Camera Calibration
  • Perform Wanding
  • Finish Calibration

Prepare for Camera Calibration

  1. In the Motive software, locate the Camera Calibration area and do the following:
    1. Select the Calibration option at the top.
    2. In the Options section, expand the Calibration Options area, and select Full from the Calibration Type drop-down.

      For example:

    3. Click Mask Visible.

Perform Wanding

Finish Calibration

  1. Continue with the instructions to calibrate the Ground Plane.

    2. The ground plane must be calibrated again after a full camera calibration to ensure the Dimension tracks correctly.
