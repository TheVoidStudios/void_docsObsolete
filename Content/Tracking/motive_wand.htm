TED 2017 Blaster

For TED 2017, The VOID brought the Ghostbusters experience. However, a small adjustment had to be made for TED. Guns were not permitted. A special blaster (wand) was created for TED.

Defining the wand rigid body is similar to the gun rigid body with these exceptions:

  • Name the wands as 'WAND 2##'; where # is the two-digit sequential number. For example, the first Wand is WAND 201.
  • When setting the pivot point, the temporary marker is placed at the top.
